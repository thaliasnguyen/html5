The Article Area

Stdio Ponoc is a Japanese animation studio based in Musashino, Tokyo, Japan. The company was founded by former Studio Ghibli lead film producer Yoshiaki Nishimura in April 2015.

Section Area I

Its first feature film Mary and the Witch's Flower was released on July 8, 2017, throughout Japan. Modest Heroes was released in 2018.

Section Area II

Yoshiaki Nishimura founded Studio Ponoc on April 15, 2015, and gained the support of several animators previously working under Studio Ghibli, including director Hiromasa Yonebayashi. The studio worked on an advertisement for JR West for its 2015 summer campaign.
